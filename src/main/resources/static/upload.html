On-line Digital Voting Administration

Click on the button to upload a spreadsheet listing the people who should be allowed to vote. The spreadsheet should be text-only, tab-delimited; i.e. don't try to upload an Excel document as-is, export to tab-delimited and upload that.
There should be exactly two columns: email address, and name (real-person name not login name).
Accounts will be created for people who don't have accounts.
